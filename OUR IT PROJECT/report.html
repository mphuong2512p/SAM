III) Project Description

1. Overview

Topic

Due to the increased demand for a new technology that can completely solve many compulsory subjects in school, for instance, Math or Physics, only by scanning, entering files with those problems or even finishing this stage vocally. Furthermore, due to the inability to provide accurate and quick answers in the current existing applications. We aim to develop a single app to assist students with their academic work.

This project aims to develop cutting-edge technology that will only be used by Vietnamese high school students, such as a mobile phone app or other high-tech devices. This application uses the latest technological advancements and artificial intelligence (AI). The end result will include a full justification for every response and every question on every topic taught in high school.

Motivation

Our distinct high school educational experiences served as our inspiration for working on this project. We found that the latest version of AI-based learning tools, such as QANDA, Dicamon, and PhotoMath, do not allow us the flexibility to enter the questions. Additionally, the algorithms used in those programs only look for previously solved answers and a propensity to interpret input questions incorrectly. Additionally, we examine, evaluate, and then apply into our invention some modern IT trends, particularly integrated AI-assistant features to answer, help with foreign language subjects, and Intelligent Recommendation will offer personalized solutions based on the users' strengths, preferences, and potential improvement areas[1]. Finally, we think that not only pupils but also individuals in need may find our website valuable. Developing the website will require a wide range of skills: web programming, collecting user experience, soft skills, project management and collaboration. The concept of AI-based learning is not new, but our solution will be the most thorough and sophisticated AI-based learning currently on the market to attract potential employers.

Landscape

Since this concept has been extremely popular due to the advance in artificial intelligence, we acknowledge that several problems will occur when we choose and develop it. Several significant rivals: QANDA (https://qanda.ai/vi)[2], Dicamon (https://dicamon.vn)[3], PhotoMath (https://photomath.com/en)[4] have created products that are comparable to ours. The QANDA website, developed by Mathpresso Inc., served as the most intelligent to scan math problems and provide detailed solutions. In a similar way, the websites for Dicamon and PhotoMath use the same methods that use optical character recognition to answer the questions. These websites provide numerous options for a single question so that students can select the most appropriate ones as they help students in their educational path. After examining the competitors' advantages: offer a mobile application, a user-friendly interface, and a large answer database. Our main objective is to create an AI assistant to help students in grades 10, 11, and 12. The website will provide consumers a variety of features and functions while upholding a user-friendly interface and designs as a result of our virtual assistant's logical and imaginative training. This has made the project created by our team stand out from the competition in the market.

2. Detailed Description

a. Aims

In recent years, learning has gotten more challenging and competitive, while technology has advanced and improved. Since then, there has been an increase in demand from students for educational answer tools that would be integrated into computers and smartphones. Additionally, the AI-based learning systems that are currently accessible are unable to fully resolve and provide a clear explanation. After fully comprehending user requirements, our goal is to develop new AI-based learning with the most advanced capabilities. Hence, there would be a potential for our team to develop a new technology that will be the most advanced and comprehensive aid for Vietnamese pupils.

In order to compete with other platforms, we first conducted a poll on people's opinions of AI-based learning to identify the applications' shortcomings. This approach has produced outcomes with a wide range of user viewpoints; some features require improvement or full resolution. Two of our standout features are the AI assistant and the thorough explanation. Students will benefit from having adequate preparation before any test or exam. Our application also allows users to enter questions using any format they like, including text, scanning, photos, or voice. The users will save time and effort. As a result of this, our application may confidently compete consistently with other platforms.

After the planning phase, we decided to collect data and consult with the seniors about the viability. This is the most important stage that we need to go over. We have to divide our task among three people in order to research related techniques and schedule a consultation with our guidance. The development of the application demo on the GitHub repository then begins. We believe that the earlier a project is started, the more issues will be resolved quickly.

The User Interface's final objective is to smoothly satisfy users' needs, provide a multi-functional interface, help users understand the entire website, and perform other technical tasks. Due to the fact that it is essential to any application's success. Sketch, wireframe, mockup, and prototype are the four processes we will take to create the interface in order to showcase a well-designed application.

IV) Skills and Jobs

Due to the lack of employees working on it at the moment, it would be a tremendous change if venture capitalists granted us the option to extend this project. This would improve the precision and efficiency of the development process. Technical capabilities and soft skills make up the two sections of the hiring requirements. We are searching for dependable and skilled personnel to help us achieve our long-term business objective. The project should carry out the activities listed in this development plan, which include establishing an AI assistant, a three-month schedule, gathering data, and building the model.

Our SAM project already has 4 positions in charge of Project Manager, Quality Tester and Technical Advisor. We maintain the consistency of the system and generate project ideas and technical functions. Technically speaking, we are looking for candidates for four positions: backend developers, a data analyst, and two software engineers.

Back-end Developer

The ability to build up the server and database structure, specifically choosing and configuring the right technological stack, is necessary for this position's members. Additionally, we are searching for someone in this role who has strengths in building API. The tasks listed below fall under the purview of a back-end developer:

  • Have a solid understanding of performance requirements and objectives, to build high-efficient solutions.
  • Create and manage application programming interfaces (APIs).
  • Create storage and acceptance solutions for websites, especially those that process payments.
  • Entails writing, testing, and maintaining development solutions for issues with code.

A person with experience as a DevOps Engineer or Software Engineer is advantageous. We nevertheless appreciate ambitious members who have no fear of making errors and learn from them.

Data Analyst

In this project, we required an individual who has the ability to translate the raw data into logical insights. Ensuring the accuracy and quality of the core data. That will contribute to providing comprehensible illustrations for our users and stakeholders. The essential skills that we are looking for as a data analyst:

  • Correcting coding issues and other relevant issues, and removing corrupted data.
  • Rearranging data in a legible way through the creation and upkeep of databases and data systems.
  • Filter data by looking into reports and performance metrics to find and fix coding issues.
  • The diagnosis and prognosis could benefit from using statistical methods to find, examine, and interpret patterns and trends in complex data sets.

The ideal candidate will have good communication and presentation skills as well as proficiency with data analytic tools including R, Python, and SQL. This will eventually provide a strong rigor for the project.

Two AI Software Engineers

An employee who fills this role through recruiting is in charge of testing and qualifying as well as artificial intelligence (machine learning, deep learning). Two employees will be hired for this position in order to increase productivity and reduce workload strain. Due to the lack of experience and expertise on our team in developing AI-based learning products. An AI software engineer's primary responsibility in this project is to productize data science research so that it can benefit internal stakeholders or external clients. Additionally, an AI software engineer is in charge of keeping up with the possibilities for transferring our technology, a significant undertaking that includes:

  • Build Infrastructure as Code
  • Continuous Integration and Versioning Control
  • Tests
  • API Development
  • Development of Pilots and MVP Applications

In general, we are searching for a highly skilled candidate with experience in the field of software engineering, problem-solving skills and a high tolerance for stress are also needed.
